About Us

The History of A.I.C. Litein Girls' High School

A.I.C Litein Girls' High School is a Girls only boarding Secondary School, located in Bureti Constituency near Litein Town, Bureti Constituency in Kericho County; within the Rift Valley Region of Kenya.

The school started as a missionary school by the A.I.C. missionaries for the Litein community in the year 2003. During its initiation, it only had two dormitories, four classes and 10 teachers.

Fast forward to 2022 and the school has vastly developed with five dormitories, including a four storey dormitory building, a two storey library, 16 classrooms and 50 teachers, both TSA and PTA employed.

Aside from teaching the 8-4-4 curriculum system to our childern, we instill religious growth, development and discipline to lead them further in their adult lives.
